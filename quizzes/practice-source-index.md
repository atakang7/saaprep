## Avoid

Do not use or copy sources that advertise:

- real exam questions
- dumps
- verified current exam bank
- guaranteed pass through memorized questions
- paid PDFs reposted on document-sharing sites

These are usually inaccurate, unethical, or copyright-problematic. They also train memorization instead of the architecture judgment the exam actually tests.
